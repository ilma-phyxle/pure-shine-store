
# GRAPC CLEANING SUPPLIES — E-Commerce Website

## Brand & Design System
- **Color palette**: Deep blue (trust/professionalism), fresh green (clean/eco), white (cleanliness), with soft grays for backgrounds
- **Style**: Minimal, spacious layout with soft shadows, rounded cards, smooth hover animations
- **Typography**: Clean sans-serif fonts with strong hierarchy

---

## 1. Homepage
- **Hero Section**: Bold headline ("Commercial & Residential Cleaning Supplies"), subtext, prominent "Shop Now" CTA, professional cleaning imagery background
- **Trust Badges**: Free shipping, Australian-owned, bulk pricing, fast delivery icons
- **Featured Categories**: Visual grid of ~12 product categories (Floor Care, Bathroom, Kitchen, Disinfectants, Paper Products, Janitorial Equipment, Hand Hygiene, Laundry, Glass & Surface, Waste Management, Outdoor, Specialty Chemicals)
- **Best-Selling Products**: Carousel/grid of top products with prices, quick-add-to-cart
- **About Section**: Brief company story, mission, and value propositions
- **Testimonials**: Customer reviews with ratings and business names
- **CTA Banner**: Bold section encouraging bulk orders or account signups

## 2. Shop Page
- **Filter Sidebar**: Filter by category, price range, brand, and product type
- **Sorting Options**: Price (low/high), popularity, newest, name
- **Product Grid**: Clean card layout with image, name, price, rating, and add-to-cart button
- **Pagination**: Load more or paginated navigation

## 3. Product Detail Page
- **Image Gallery**: Multiple product images with zoom
- **Product Info**: Name, price, description, size/variant selector, quantity picker, add-to-cart
- **Specifications Tab**: Detailed specs (volume, ingredients, usage instructions)
- **Reviews Tab**: Customer reviews with star ratings
- **Related Products**: Grid of similar/complementary items

## 4. Cart & Checkout (Shopify-Powered)
- **Shopping Cart**: Slide-out cart drawer with item list, quantity adjusters, subtotal
- **Checkout**: Powered by Shopify's secure checkout flow

## 5. Additional Pages
- **About Us**: Company story, team, values
- **Contact**: Contact form, phone, email, address
- **Header**: Logo, navigation (Home, Shop, About, Contact), cart icon with item count
- **Footer**: Links, social media, newsletter signup

## 6. Shopify Integration
- A new Shopify development store will be created (no Shopify payments needed while building)
- Demo products will populate the catalog across 10-15 categories
- You'll receive a 30-day free Shopify trial when you claim the store; a paid plan is needed after that to sell

## 7. Responsive Design
- Fully responsive across desktop, tablet, and mobile
- Mobile hamburger menu, touch-friendly product cards, optimized checkout flow
